AI-powered content refresh. Keep old posts ranking by updating them in your own writing voice.

== Description ==

Most site owners post new content and forget about old posts. But old posts already have backlinks, authority, and Google trust. Leseo analyzes your existing writing style, then uses AI to improve your old content while keeping your voice intact.

= How It Works =

1. **Analyze** — The plugin reads your recent posts and learns your writing style, tone, and structure.
2. **Revive** — Pick an old post. AI rewrites it with fresh information, better depth, and your voice.
3. **Create** — Need new content? Generate posts from scratch using your established persona.
4. **Publish** — Review the output, then publish directly or save as draft.

= Why Refresh Old Content? =

* Old posts with backlinks already have ranking power
* Real improvements signal to Google the content got better
* Existing links amplify the update — no new outreach needed
* AI writes in your voice, not a generic tone
* Faster than writing from scratch

= Features =

* **Auto-discover persona** — Analyze your posts to automatically build a writing profile
* **Revive old posts** — Select any published post and get a refreshed version
* **Create new posts** — Generate content from scratch using your voice
* **Diff view** — See exactly what changed between old and new content
* **Writing rules** — Define custom style guidelines the AI follows
* **Multi-language** — Generate content in 18+ languages
* **Usage tracking** — Monitor your monthly AI call limits

= Important =

For best results, set your writing rules in Settings and review the post before publishing. AI-generated content should always be reviewed before publishing. 

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `leseo` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/` or install via the WordPress plugin directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress admin.
3. Go to **Leseo > Settings** and enter your API key.
4. Click **Analyze from Posts** to auto-discover your writing persona.
5. Start reviving old posts or creating new ones.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need an API key? =

Yes. Leseo connects to a cloud AI service. Get your free API key at [leseo.app](https://leseo.app).

= Does it publish automatically? =

You choose. After AI generates content, you can publish directly or save as draft to review and edit first.

= What languages are supported? =

English, German, Polish, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Japanese, Chinese, Arabic, Turkish, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Finnish, Czech, Romanian, and Hungarian.

= Is my content sent to third parties? =

Content is sent to the Leseo AI service for processing. It is not stored, shared, or used for training. See our privacy policy for details.

= Can I customize the writing style? =

Yes. Go to Settings and define your persona and writing rules. You can also auto-discover these from your existing posts.
